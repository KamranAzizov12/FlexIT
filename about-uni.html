about ASOIU

The rise of what became ASOIU is tied to the rise of the petroleum industry in the Baku region. By 1887 the preparatory technical school that would become Baku Polytechnicum was established in Baku. The new school focused on training engineers in a broad range of industries, from agriculture to oil. By 1923 the school graduated its first three students; by 1927 it had risen to 289 graduates.In September 2015, by order of President Ilham Aliyev, the University was named the Azerbaijan State Oil and Industrial University, which was tasked with training qualified personnel not only for the oil field but also for the entire domestic industry.

IT club

AzII-IT Club provides free IT trainings for university students. Oracle Academy, Cisco Academy and Microsoft Academy of international level are registered within this club.

Magnat club

The intellectual club “Magnat” consists of teams that take part in tournaments such as “Who? Where? When?”, “Brain ring”, “Hamsa”, as well as systematically provides the organization of intellectual competitions inside of the university.
